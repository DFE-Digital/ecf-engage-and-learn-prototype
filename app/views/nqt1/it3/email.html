Extra support for teachers who had their statutory induction disrupted by the pandemic

Hello,

Are there teachers at your school who missed out on having a normal statutory induction because of the pandemic?

We are offering 2 things to help them during the next academic year:

  • free online resources to develop their skills and knowledge
  • a one-off payment to go towards giving them extra development time

We appreciate it comes late in the day, but hope these can be added to existing plans for them.

How to get the optional resources

Schools can choose resources from one of 4 DfE-accredited providers:

https://ecf-engage-and-learn-prototype.herokuapp.com/nqt1/it3/main

This link will only work for your school.

If you have any questions, reply to this email.
