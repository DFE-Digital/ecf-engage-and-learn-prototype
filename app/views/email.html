Hi Joe,

Congratulations on becoming a teacher.

Teachers who complete their Initial Teacher Training from 2021 onwards will now do a 2-year induction once they start their first teaching role.

This is to help teachers get the best possible start to their careers, enhancing their skills so they have the tools they need to be successful.

At the end of their induction teachers will have their performance assessed to ensure they are meeting the Teachers’ Standards.

The training is broken down into weekly topics that teachers can access online. The themes they cover are closely related to those in the Teachers’s Standards, so there’ll be no nasty surprises when they are assessed.

Teachers will be supported throughout by a mentor, and will also be encouraged to join online group sessions, webinars and ‘buddy’ programmes with other new teachers.
