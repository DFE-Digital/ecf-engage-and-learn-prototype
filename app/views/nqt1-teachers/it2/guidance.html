What’s available

This service gives you access to the same resources teachers doing the new early career training programme use.

We hope these materials will help fill knowledge and skills gaps over the next academic year.

Your school should also have arranged for you to have some extra development time away from the classroom to use on statutory induction training.

How to use these resources

These materials feature a wide range of theory and practical classroom techniques aligned to the teachers’ standards.

Unlike teachers doing the new programme, who use these materials throughout their 2-year induction, you will only have access for 3 terms.

To get the best out of them you should:

  • focus on development areas disrupted by the pandemic. Do not try to cover all the material
  • dip in and out of the resources rather than following the prescribed week by week structure

Note that you should ignore references to mentor meetings and associated practical exercises featured in the resources. These only apply to teachers doing the new programme.

We encourage you to keep reflections and notes while using these materials. These will be a useful reference, and evidence for your progress reviews.
