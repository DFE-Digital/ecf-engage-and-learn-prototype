Hello,

Was your statutory induction training disrupted because of the pandemic?

Your school has created a GOV.UK account where you can access materials designed to help you hone your teaching skills and knowledge.

It’s entirely optional, but the idea is that you focus on any development areas you missed out on during the pandemic.

You can use the materials now until the summer of 2022.

Why you’ve been selected

We are specifically offering help to teachers at state-funded schools who either:

  • completed their induction in the 2020 to 2021 academic year

Or

  • had started but not yet completed their induction by 1 September 2021

To sign in to your account, go to:

http://support-for-early-career-teachers.education.gov.uk/start

You will need the email address your school used when they created your account

The Teacher Continuing Professional Development team
