Choose support materials for 2020 cohort teachers

You can choose from 4 options. They all cover the same theory and practical classroom techniques aligned to the teachers’ standards. You should choose the one that best fits your school’s needs.

These materials were designed for early career teachers doing the new 2-year induction programme, so you might find this cohort has already covered some of the content. We therefore recommend focusing on development areas that were disrupted by the pandemic.

Ambition Institute

Modules are broken down into topics featuring:

  • evidence summaries
  • videos of experts talking through their insights and how these work in practice
  • a quiz to check teachers have understood the evidence summary
  • questions for teachers to discuss with their mentors
  • evidence summaries

Education Development Trust

Modules are broken down into topics featuring:

  • information to show how evidence supports education research and its implications on teaching
  • practical ideas to try in the classroom
  • methods teachers can use to reflect on their development and identify ways to improve

Teach First

Modules are broken down into topics featuring:

  • video tutorials from education experts
  • case studies
  • questions they can then discuss with their mentor

UCL Early Career Teacher Consortium

Modules are broken down into topics featuring:

  • evidence from research
  • practical skills to learn from case studies
  • reflections on what they’ve learnt

If you’re still not sure, you can contact us for more information.
